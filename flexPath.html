Learning Path To Creating Any Layout Using Flexbox

The list contains all of the best resources, taken from various platforms and websites, to make you understand the ins and outs of Flexbox. Some of the concepts might repeat themselves in the subsequent links but make sure to not skip anything. it will help you in the reinforcement of concepts.

Initially, I used to struggle a lot when it came to making layouts and positioning things where I want them to be. I didn't know where to start. For some, it's easy as they are naturally good at it but some people struggle. By the end of this path, you'll feel comfortable making any kind of layout.

It's very important to build the foundational knowledge of how things are working. It is only then that you'll get comfortable fixing errors and finding why some styles are not working as they should be working even though you're writing everything correctly. Believe me, this happens a lot! That is why I haven't just jumped into Flexbox right away.

Don't try to memorize everything. Feel free to come and reference anything at any time later. This is the correct way of doing things.
